Receipt capture

Scan the receipt.
Start from the real bill.

Upload a photo from your camera roll or scan it fresh. IOU keeps the receipt, tax, tip, and final total in one flow, so the split starts from the bill instead of someone's memory.

  • Upload an existing photo or scan with your camera
  • Adjust subtotal, tax, tip, and additional charges
  • Every line item stays tied to the original bill
Item assignment

Every item,
to the right person.

Assign dishes to one person, a few people, or everyone at the table. IOU keeps the split connected to what the group actually ordered instead of flattening everything into one average.

  • Tap each item and pick exactly who shared it
  • Switch between equal, exact, percent, or adjusted splits per item
  • See each person's running total as you go
Split options

Split it
your way.

Four ways to split — equal, exact dollar amounts, percentages, or adjusted — so the split always matches the real situation, not just the lazy default.

  • Equal split among whoever ordered the item
  • Exact amounts when people chip in specific dollars
  • Percent or adjust for custom arrangements
Groups

One group.
All your shared expenses.

Create a group for a trip, a house, a recurring dinner crew, or whatever keeps bringing the same people back together. Add friends or send an invite link, and every expense stays attached to the running balance.

  • Add friends or send a link to join from their phone
  • Live balances visible on the group page at all times
  • Settle up directly from the group — no digging through chats
